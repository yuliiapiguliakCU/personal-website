My Hobbies

Computer

Computers became interesting during my school years. Now I study, experiment with technology, and code for personal enjoyment and growth. Studying computer science aligns perfectly with my hobby of computers, providing a structured way to learn and understand the digital realm.

Lego

My journey into the world of LEGO as a hobby started in adulthood, inspired by beloved movie franchises like Harry Potter and Star Wars. These iconic building sets rekindled my passion for imaginative play and construction, making it a joyful and fulfilling pastime. LEGO is a way to relax from work and school.

Travel

My passion for travel began during my school years when my parents took me to Egypt, marking the beginning of a love for exploring new places and cultures. Since then, I've had visited countries like Canada, Colombia, Costa Rica, Mexico, Turkey, and others, all of which have enriched my life with diverse experiences and unforgettable memories.
